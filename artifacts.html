Key Questions & Insights:

1. Most Impactful Elements

The ongoing struggle for recognition and preservation of Ainu culture resonated deeply, showing how historical discrimination continues to impact present-day communities.

2. Contemporary Discrimination

Learning about current discrimination faced by the Ainu people opened my eyes to how prejudice persists in modern societies, even in advanced nations.

3. Cultural Preservation Efforts

The determination of Ainu people to maintain their traditions while adapting to modern life demonstrates remarkable resilience.

Oral Presentation Experience 🎤

Let's talk about my presentation journey - where nervous energy met determination!

Before the Presentation

"The night before, I was convinced I'd rather watch paint dry than speak in front of the class!" 😅

  • Practiced in front of my mirror (probably confused my family)
  • Reviewed my notes until they were practically memorized
  • Tried to remember that everyone else was probably nervous too ❌
During the Presentation

Reality check: While I wasn't exactly channeling my inner TED Talk speaker...

🎯 Managed to get through it without passing out (success!)

💪 Hands were shaking, but voice stayed relatively steady

🌟 Actually remembered my key points ❌

After the Presentation

Looking back, it wasn't as terrible as I imagined:

  • Realized I knew my material better than I thought
  • Found out public speaking doesn't actually kill you
  • Gained confidence for future presentations
  • Proud that I pushed through my comfort zone

"Sure, I wasn't perfect, but I did it - and that's what matters! Next time will be even better." 🚀
